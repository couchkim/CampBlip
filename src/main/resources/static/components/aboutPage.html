Marshall (14) - Charlotte Latin School

When I was nine, I attended a professional Lego camp at a local school. I told my mom that they weren't doing anything we couldn't do at our own house. That winter, we had our first camp session, a two-day holiday camp in December. With my mom's help, we organized the sets, created a catalog, designed t-shirts, and invited campers. We now have grown into a summer camp with multiple sessions. I have learned how to employ and manage people, budget, and more. We even donate some of our proceeds to charities like Susan G. Komen and the Levine Children's Hospital. One of my favorite parts of running camp is watching campers come back every year. Some have even become counselors. My favorite Lego set is Frank Lloyd Wright's Robie House.
