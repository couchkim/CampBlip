Interested in a Camp Blip Birthday Party?

Marshall and a member of his counselor staff will organize 2 hours of Lego fun for your child and his/her friends.

What's Included?

  • A variety of sets based on your child's theme preferences
  • (An average of 2 sets per attendee)
  • Large tub of Legos for "free build"
  • Custom keepsake party catalog that includes the available party sets

Pricing

  • $100 for up to 10 children
  • $10 per child over 10 attendees
